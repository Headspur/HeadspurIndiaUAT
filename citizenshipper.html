About CitizenShipper

CitizenShipper connects people who need to transport their beloved pets and most important, hard-to-ship items — like cars, motorcycles and furniture — with trusted and reliable transportation professionals. Our online marketplace makes it fast and affordable for shipping customers to find the ideal driver to match their needs, budget and schedule.

CRO Report

Conversion Optimization, or Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO), consists of a set of coordinated actions with the sole objective of increasing, as much as possible, the number of visitors to a site that completes a certain action (or conversion) that generates value for the business. ➔ Conversion Optimization efforts only achieve positive results when it's data-driven.
