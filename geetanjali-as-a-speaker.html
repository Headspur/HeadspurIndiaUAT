About

Geetanjali has always had a knack for finding creative solutions to problems. With over a decade of experience in the digital space, she has helped brands – including start-ups – scale up their sales and marketing efforts. She is an expert in sales, marketing and commercial planning, and has successfully launched products and businesses in both the B2C and B2B spaces. Geetanjali consults with businesses to help them enhance their digital presence and achieve their ambitious goals. With her expertise, brands have been able to achieve targets such as 3X revenue growth and expanding their businesses overseas.

Geetanjali has a wealth of experience spanning over 13 years in a number of different industries. She's worked in publishing, e-commerce, real estate, consulting and travel aggregators - giving her a unique insight into how to craft an effective web presence for brands. Her expertise has helped clients like Robin Sharma, Melanie Notkins, Ola, Accor Hotels, Motorola and many others achieve their user acquisition and revenue growth goals. Geetanjali is known for her smart wit and engaging writing style - making her the perfect person to helm your brand's web presence.
